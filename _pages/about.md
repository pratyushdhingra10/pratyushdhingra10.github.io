I am a PhD student in Computer Engineering at Washington State University.

My research focuses on heterogeneous architectures, memory-centric computing, and energy-efficient machine learning systems. I am interested in building hardware-software co-designed solutions that improve performance and efficiency for modern AI workloads.

On this website, you can find:

- Publications and preprints
- Talks and presentations
- Teaching and mentoring activities
- Projects and professional updates

I am always open to research collaborations and technical discussions in computer architecture, ML systems, and accelerator design.
